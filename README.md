# Prototype Usage Notes (Concise)

## 1. Scan a Bag (Home)
**How to trigger:**
- Tap the `Scan a Bag` button in the top Hero section.
- Or tap the `Supports Amara` label on a product card. Both open the same modal card.

**What you will see:**
- A farmer detail modal that includes:
  - **Tree Map & Traceability (collapsible):** a real map background with the farmer region and Tree #14 pins.
  - **Tree Live:** a black live-view frame with a play button (visual only).
  - **Tree Timeline:** a vertical timeline of the tree’s growth (scrollable within the section).
  - **Support a Tree:** the tipping entry point.

**What it means:**
- This card concentrates “traceability + impact + participation” into a single view so the user can understand and act without leaving the flow.
- The collapsible map and timeline make origin and lifecycle easy to scan.

---

## 2. Community Page
**Entry:** Tap `Community` in the top navigation.

**What’s inside:**
- **Post box:** UI for creating a post (visual only).
- **Stories:** latest updates with like, comment, and share actions.
- **Comments (expandable):** tap the comment button on a story to reveal its comments and input.

**What it means:**
- Builds a sense of shared participation and social proof.
- Expandable comments create lightweight interaction without overwhelming the feed.

---

## 3. Impact Page
**Entry:** Tap `Impact` in the top navigation.

**What’s inside:**
- **Personal summary:** total tips and trees supported.
- **Your Trees list:** farmers/trees you’ve supported.
- **Unlockables:** milestone rewards (e.g., video unlocks).
- **Blockchain verification:** opens a verification screen (visual only).

**What it means:**
- Summarizes the user’s impact to reinforce retention and pride.
- Turns “supporting a tree” into a tangible, trackable contribution.
